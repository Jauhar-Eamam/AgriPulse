# 🌾 AgriPulse – Smart Agritech Platform

KrishiSetu is a unified agritech platform designed to empower farmers by providing intelligent insights and easy access to essential agricultural resources such as climate data, market prices, government schemes, and modern farming technologies.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Agriculture faces multiple challenges including climate uncertainty, lack of market transparency, limited awareness of government schemes, and low adoption of modern technologies.

KrishiSetu addresses these challenges by building an integrated platform that simplifies decision-making and improves accessibility for farmers.

---

## 🎯 Features

### 🌦️ Climate Insights 
- Real-time weather data
- Seasonal predictions
- Alerts for extreme conditions

---

### 📈 Market Intelligence 
- Live mandi (market) prices
- Crop price trends
- Better selling decisions for farmers

---

### 🏛️ Government Scheme Assistance 
- Personalized scheme recommendations
- Simple explanation of schemes
- Eligibility-based filtering
- Required documents & application steps

---

### 🚜 Modern Farming Awareness 
- Information about latest technologies
- Tools and best practices
- Guidance for improving productivity

---

## 🧠 Key Innovation

- Personalized recommendation engine for schemes
- Integration of climate + market + policy data
- Farmer-friendly UI with simple language
- Scalable architecture for future automation

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Farmer enters basic details:
   - Location
   - Crop type
   - Land size
   - Income

2. System processes:
   - Weather data
   - Market trends
   - Eligibility criteria

3. Output:
   - Relevant government schemes
   - Market insights
   - Farming recommendations
